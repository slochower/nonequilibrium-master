CC       oligomers that cause cell rounding (PubMed:22898822). The toxin
CC       can be highly efficient at very low concentrations by acting on
CC       formin homology family proteins: toxic actin oligomers bind with

CC       elongation abilities of formins, causing their potent inhibition

CC       similarity). Acts as an acid--amino-acid ligase that transfers the
